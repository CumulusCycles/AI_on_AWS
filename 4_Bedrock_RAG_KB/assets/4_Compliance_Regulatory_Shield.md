# AI Agent Insure
## Compliance & Regulatory Shield

### Product Overview
Provides protection against AI governance enforcement, audits, algorithmic accountability reviews, and insurable civil penalties.

### Primary Risk Exposures
- AI Act investigation
- Algorithmic bias inquiry
- Data protection audit
- Governance documentation gaps

### Core Coverage Components
- Regulatory defense costs
- Audit response expense
- Compliance remediation funding
- Insurable civil penalty reimbursement

### Underwriting Criteria
- Cross-border regulatory exposure
- Governance framework maturity
- Documentation transparency

### Coverage Triggers
- Formal investigation notice
- Written allegation of governance violation

### Key Exclusions
- Criminal penalties
- Willful misconduct

### Claim Scenarios
- EU AI Act compliance investigation
- Data authority alleges discriminatory model bias
