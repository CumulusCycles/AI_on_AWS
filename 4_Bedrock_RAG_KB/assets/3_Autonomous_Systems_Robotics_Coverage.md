# AI Agent Insure
## Autonomous Systems & Robotics Coverage

### Product Overview
Covers bodily injury and property damage caused by AI-driven physical systems including robotics, drones, vehicles, and industrial automation.

### Primary Risk Exposures
- Sensor drift
- Navigation miscalibration
- Robotic arm misalignment
- Drone crash
- Autonomous vehicle collision
- Industrial automation malfunction

### Core Coverage Components
- Bodily injury liability
- Property damage liability
- Product-completed operations
- Firmware and AI attribution protection
- Downtime extension

### Underwriting Criteria
- Manual override presence
- Safety interlock systems
- Hardware-software integration review
- Environmental testing documentation

### Coverage Triggers
- Physical injury tied to AI decision error
- Property damage caused by system malfunction

### Key Exclusions
- Mechanical wear unrelated to AI
- Unauthorized modification
- Operation outside approved scope

### Claim Scenarios
- Warehouse robot injures employee
- Autonomous drone damages third-party property
