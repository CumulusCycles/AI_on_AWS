# AI Agent Insure
## AI Incident Response & Crisis Management

### Product Overview
Provides rapid-response funding and coordination following high-visibility AI failure events or reputational crises.

### Primary Risk Exposures
- Viral harmful output
- Bias exposure event
- Public regulatory scrutiny
- Customer trust erosion

### Core Coverage Components
- Public relations advisory funding
- Legal crisis counsel
- Customer notification costs
- Technical containment support

### Underwriting Criteria
- Media exposure risk
- User base scale
- Crisis simulation preparedness

### Coverage Triggers
- Public disclosure of AI harm event
- Regulatory inquiry following incident

### Key Exclusions
- Reputation loss without defined triggering event

### Claim Scenarios
- Viral AI output causes reputational crisis
- Public backlash following biased model behavior
