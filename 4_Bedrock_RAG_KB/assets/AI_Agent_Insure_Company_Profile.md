# 1. Company Overview

AI Agent Insure is a specialty insurance organization focused exclusively on risks arising from artificial intelligence systems, autonomous agents, machine learning infrastructure, and AI-driven decision-making platforms.

The company is designed as an AI-native insurer rather than a traditional cyber or technology E&O provider.

AI Agent Insure provides tailored insurance solutions addressing operational, regulatory, financial, physical, and reputational risks created by modern AI systems, including:

- Agentic AI
- Robotics and autonomous systems
- Synthetic data environments
- Large-scale model deployments
